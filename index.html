Circuit project

This was more of a physical project than a coding project. It involved me going through multiple planning steps to build a karaoke machine that used an aux cord to play music and dials that controlled the volume, tone, and acted as a mixer. The board also included LEDs that displayed the volume, which was also controlled on the PCB board.

  • Hand-drawn schematic: Calculating the values of the resistors, potentiometers, and capacitors.
  • Simulation: Using Multisim to test the circuit design.
  • Breadboard test build: Assembling the circuit on a breadboard for initial testing.
  • Final assembly: Soldering the components onto a PCB board to complete the final product.
